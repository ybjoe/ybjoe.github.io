Hello, I'm Yongbaek Cho, a Software Engineer at CZI - Imaging Institute. My passion for software engineering, AI, and machine learning drives my work across diverse interdisciplinary domains. At CZI, I utilize my expertise to collaborate with colleagues, tackling a range of data-related challenges and pioneering creative solutions. Prior to this, I was part of the data team at Linqia.

My academic background includes a Master's degree in Computer Science from Arizona State University, where I specialized in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence under the guidance of Dr. Yezhou Yang, co-advised by Dr. Yi Ren. My research interests center around Computer Vision, Adversarial Attacks, and Generative Models. 
Specifically, I'm dedicated to fortifying synthetic content generated by generative models against adversarial perturbations. 
This involves integrating attributional watermarks into speech generative models such as Text-to-Speech synthesis models and voice conversion systems.
I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer and Information Science from the University of Arizona, which provided a solid technical foundation and ignited my passion for the field.
